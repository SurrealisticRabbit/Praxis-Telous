# Praxis Telous

> *Praxis (Action) + Telos (Goal/End)*

A structured task management application designed for people who have big ideas but struggle with procrastination. It provides a hierarchical way to collate thoughts and break them down into actionable steps.

## Core Philosophy

The goal of Praxis Telous is to provide a space to capture everything from fleeting ideas to fully-fledged projects without feeling overwhelmed. The workflow is designed to move concepts through distinct stages:

1.  **Capture Ideas**: Anything can start as an **Idea**. This is a low-friction way to log thoughts, links, or random sparks of inspiration before they're lost. They don't belong to any project yet.
2.  **Develop into Projects**: When an idea gains momentum, it can be promoted into a **Project**. A project is a high-level goal or a significant endeavor.
3.  **Break Down into Tasks**: Projects are made manageable by breaking them down into smaller, concrete **Tasks**. Each task can be further divided into **Subtasks**.

This structure helps you organize chaos, see the big picture, and focus on the immediate next step.

## Features

*   **Hierarchical Structure**: Organize your work into **Projects**, **Tasks**, and **Subtasks**.
*   **Flexible Status Tracking**: Guide items through your workflow with customizable statuses. Start with `Idea`, move to `To Do`, `In Progress`, and finally `Done`.
*   **Visual Organization**: Customize projects and tasks with specific **Icons** and **Colors** to make them visually distinct and easy to identify.
*   **Rich Details**: Add detailed descriptions to tasks to keep all context in one place.
